About Kaprisan Holidays

Kaprisan Holidays Co. Ltd. is a leading name in the Indian travel and tourism industry, proudly catering to wanderers and explorers from every corner of the country. Headquartered in India, our mission is simple — to make travel seamless, memorable, and meaningful for everyone.

Founded with a passion for exploration and a commitment to excellence, Kaprisan Holidays specializes in curating customized travel experiences across India. Whether it’s a serene hill station retreat, a spiritual pilgrimage, an adventurous wildlife escape, or a heritage-packed cultural tour—we’ve got you covered.

With years of industry experience and deep-rooted travel expertise, we pride ourselves on offering end-to-end solutions that include:
Tailored holiday packages
Hotel and resort bookings
Flight and train reservations
Guided tours and local experiences
Corporate travel planning
Honeymoon and group trips

What sets us apart

What sets us apart is our customer-first approach, transparent operations, and passion for delivering exceptional service. Our dedicated team of travel professionals works around the clock to ensure your journey is smooth, safe, and truly unforgettable.


At Kaprisan Holidays Co. Ltd., we don’t just plan trips—we create experiences that stay with you for a lifetime. With a growing footprint and a loyal clientele from across India, we are proud to be your trusted travel companion.


Explore India with us—comfortably, confidently, and joyfully.
